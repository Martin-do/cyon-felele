Voting System

Every donation made through your link constitutes a vote. The number of votes is determined by the donation amount:

  • ₦500 = 1 Vote
  • ₦1,000 = 2 Votes
  • ₦5,000 = 10 Votes
  • ₦10,000 = 20 Votes

Note: There is no limit to the number of votes a supporter may cast.

Your Role

  • Share your unique campaign link with your network.
  • All donations go exclusively into the Harvest account.
  • Be an advocate for the 2026 Harvest project.
  • Winners will be celebrated at the Grand Finale!
